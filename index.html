What got you interested in IT?

My interest in IT started at my current place of work. My role involves a lot of analytical work/reporting using excel. A colleague of mine who works in our IT department introduced me to VBA. I was amazed that he was able to automate a task that would normally take me hours. From this I began taking online courses through Coursera and Udemy, learning the basics of various languages. The courses I have done so far have been in VBA, SQL, Python, HTML and CSS.


Why did you choose to come to RMIT?

I chose to study a Bachelor of Information Technology through RMIT because I would like to transition in to an IT/tech related field in the near future. At the moment I have my eyes set on data analysis or database admin, however I expect my understanding and preference of fields to change as I am exposed to different aspects of IT throughout my degree with RMIT.


What do you expect to learn during your studies?

I hope to learn more about different programming languages and to also discover other aspects of the IT industry that I may not already know about.
